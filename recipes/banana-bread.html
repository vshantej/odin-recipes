Banana Bread Recipe

This delicious banana bread recipe is pulled from Allrecipes.com. It takes an estimated 1 hour and 35 minutes to prepare and serves up to 12 people. The bread is moist, fragrant, and can be eaten as breakfast, a snack, or desert. Its basic nutritional facts are as follows:

  • 307 Calories
  • 14g of Fat
  • 44g of Carbs
  • 4g of Protein

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs, Beaten
  • 1 Cup of Mashed Bananas
  • 1/2 Cup of Vegetable Oil
  • 1/3 Cup of Buttermilk
  • 1 3/4 Cups of All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 Cups of White Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 Cup of Chopped Pecans (Optional)
  • 9-by-5 Inch Pan

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease 9-by-5 inch loaf pan.
  3. Mix eggs, bananas, oil, and buttermilk together in a large bowl until well combined.
  4. Sift flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt into a separate large bowl.
  5. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture until combined. Gently fold in pecans.
  6. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  7. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 1 hour and 20 minutes.
  8. Cut into slices and enjoy!
