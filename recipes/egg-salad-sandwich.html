Egg Salad Sandwich Recipe

Description

This recipe for egg salad sandwiches is from Allrecipes.com. Taking just 35 minutes to prepare and serving up to four people, this dish can be accompanies by a side of pickles, chips, French fries, coleslaw, or even a salad. Its nutrition facts are: 333 calories, 30 grams of fat, 2 grams of carbs, and 13 grams of protein.

Ingredients

  • 8 Hard Boiled Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of Mayonnaise
  • 1 Teaspoon of Mustard
  • 1/4 Cup of Chopped Green Onions
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper To Taste
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Boil eggs.
  3. Remove eggs from hot water: cool, peel, and chop.
  4. Combine chopped eggs in bowl with mayonnaise, green onion, and mustard.
  5. Season mixture wit paprika, salt, and pepper.
  6. Stir.
  7. Place on bread of choice and serve.
